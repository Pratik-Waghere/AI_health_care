About Health Diagnosis System

Our Mission

To provide accessible and reliable health diagnosis through advanced technology and medical expertise.

Project Overview

The Health Diagnosis System is an innovative platform that combines artificial intelligence with medical knowledge to help users understand their symptoms and get appropriate medical guidance. Our system provides:

  • Symptom-based disease prediction
  • Personalized health recommendations
  • Doctor suggestions based on symptoms
  • Health tracking and monitoring
  • Preventive care guidelines
Technology Stack
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • Database: SQLite
  • AI/ML: scikit-learn, pandas, numpy
  • Authentication: Flask-Login
  • Security: Password hashing, CSRF protection
